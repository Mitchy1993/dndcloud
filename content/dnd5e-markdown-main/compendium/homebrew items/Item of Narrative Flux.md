**Description**: Reality Altering Item. *Type chosen by player*
**Rarity**: Rare

**Item Activation**:
1. **Narrative Flux**: Once per day, as a reaction, the wearer can manipulate the fabric of reality, subtly altering minor details of the story or environment to their advantage. This could involve creating temporary cover, revealing hidden pathways, or introducing new plot elements that enhance the narrative flow. 
	- *(Will not grant or deny Nat 20's; will work, but with limited effect, on outcomes that would have a DC higher than 20)* 

**Origin**: Crafted by an enigmatic bard and sage who delved deep into the mysteries of storytelling magic, the item of Narrative Flux represents the culmination of their quest to harness the power of narrative manipulation. Infused with the essence of countless tales, the item serves as a testament to the boundless potential of the imagination.
