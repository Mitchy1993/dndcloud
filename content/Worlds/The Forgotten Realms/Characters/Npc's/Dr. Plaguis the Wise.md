### Curse of Restless Dreams
- **Description**: Those who benefit from a long rest in the witch doctor's rest stop find themselves plagued by restless dreams throughout the night. These dreams are vivid and unsettling, filled with strange visions and unsettling omens.
- **Effect**: Characters affected by the curse are Long Rested. 
	- They disadvantage on maintaining concentration on spells until they complete another long rest in a safe area.
- **Duration**: The curse lasts until they complete another long rest in a safe area, or until they seek out the witch doctor to lift the curse through a special ritual or quest.
