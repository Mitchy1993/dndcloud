Dr. Thaddeus Lockwood, an elvish physician; heart of gold beneath a rough exterior. Dr. Lockwood's journey began on the battlefield, where he served as a dedicated medic in the [[Horde War]]

Through the chaos of conflict, Dr. Lockwood honed his skills in triage, field surgery, and rapid response, saving countless lives amidst the horrors of war. His experiences instilled in him a deep sense of compassion and a relentless drive to alleviate suffering wherever he encountered it.

After the war's end, Dr. Lockwood transitioned to civilian life, bringing his expertise to the bustling streets of Waterdeep. Determined to make a difference in a world still reeling from the aftermath of conflict, Thaddeus found work at the [[House of Healing (Waterdeep)]]

Establishing himself as a physician, Dr. Lockwood quickly rose through the ranks to become the chief medical officer. His pragmatic approach to medicine, tempered by years of frontline experience, earned him the respect and admiration of both patients and colleagues alike.
